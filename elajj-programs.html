PROGRAMS.

Jiu-jitsu is for everyone. East LA Jiu Jitsu partners with community organizations to ensure that no barrier — physical, financial, or otherwise — stands between a student and the mat.

Community Partnership

East LA Regional Center Adaptive Program.

In partnership with the East LA Regional Center, we offer an adaptive jiu-jitsu program for students with developmental disabilities and special needs. Our instructors are trained to work with students at every ability level, creating a structured, supportive, and joyful experience on the mat.

This program is about more than jiu-jitsu — it's about belonging, growth, and the confidence that comes from learning something hard alongside people who believe in you.

Eligibility

This program is available to clients of the East LA Regional Center. If your child or family member receives regional center services, contact us to learn how to enroll. Scholarships are available and no student is turned away for cost.

Financial Access

Scholarship Program.

No student at East LA Jiu Jitsu is ever turned away because of cost. Our scholarship program, funded through the Ethos Jiu Jitsu Foundation, provides full and partial scholarships to students and families who need them.

There's no application process to jump through. Just talk to us — before class, after class, or by phone. We'll work something out.

  • Who qualifiesAny student or family who cannot afford full tuition
  • How to applySpeak directly with any instructor or staff member
  • FundingProvided by the Ethos Jiu Jitsu Foundation (501(c)(3))
  • AvailabilityYear-round, no waitlist
