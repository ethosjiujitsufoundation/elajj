About The Mission
East LA Jiu Jitsu is a program of the Ethos Jiu Jitsu Foundation — a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to making world-class training accessible to every kid in East Los Angeles and Boyle Heights, regardless of ability to pay.
No student is ever turned away for inability to pay.
Your Donation Supports
Scholarships — tuition for youth who can't afford to train
Equipment — gi and gear for students in need
Instructors — compensation and development
Facility — keeping the gym open for everyone
