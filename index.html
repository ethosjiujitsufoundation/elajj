What to Expect

Your first week at ELAJJ.

  • 01

    Show up. No gear needed.

    Come in comfortable clothes. We'll provide a loaner gi or let you train in sweats your first visit.

  • 02

    Meet your instructor.

    Our coaches introduce themselves, walk you through the gym, and make sure you know what to expect before you hit the mat.

  • 03

    Train with the group.

    You'll warm up, learn two or three techniques, and drill. Nobody expects perfection. Everyone started where you are.

  • 04

    Talk membership. No pressure.

    After class we'll walk you through options — including our scholarship program if cost is a concern. Nobody gets turned away.
